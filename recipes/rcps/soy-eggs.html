soy eggs~

Full recipe credits to Myo Quinn's soy-marinated eggs.

Description:

Yummy soy-marinated eggs that are great to eat with ramen or just as a snack!

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 8 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 Tbsp unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 2 dried chillies, optinal
  • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms, optional
  • Toasted sesame oil, for garnish
  • Toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

Steps

  1. bBing a medium pot of water to boil over medium high heat
  2. Gently lower the eggs into the boiling water and cook for 7 minutes
  3. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the eggs into a large bowl of ice water until cool
  4. Peel the eggs
  5. In the same medium pot add the soy sauce, 1/2 cup water, garlic, sugar, rice vinegar, chiles and shiitake mushrooms
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat
  7. Reduce heat to simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Remove pot from heat and add peeled eggs
  9. Let eggs marinade overnight
  10. To serve, slice the eggs in half
  11. Drizzle with marinade and sesame oil, and sprinkle with sesame seeds
